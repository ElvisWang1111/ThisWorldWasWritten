    Fenris Hearth-Knutson (1827–1905), also known as Fenris the Forger, was an Asterian master smith and inventor who pioneered the use of rare-earth elements in steel alloys during the 1870s. His innovations revitalized the mining economy of the Eastern Reaches and contributed to the later phase of Asteria's Industrial Awakening. A member of the prestigious Guild of Smiths, he is recognized as a key figure in the metallurgical advancements of the 19th century. The name "Fenris Hearth-Knutson" appears elsewhere in Asterian history, including the inventor of the Astrian Steam-Loom (1825) and a Grand Chamberlain of the 1920s, leading to occasional historiographical confusion.
