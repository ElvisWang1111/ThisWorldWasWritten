    Riven Sorbek (1890–1955), known by the epithet 'The Voice of the Caldon', was an Asterian poet and activist from the Caldon Mountains. Writing primarily in the Caldon language, his works advocated for the preservation and revitalisation of Caldon linguistic and cultural identity against a backdrop of assimilationist pressures in early 20th-century Asteria. Sorbek is regarded as one of the most significant literary figures of the Caldon-speaking community and a key voice in the movement for regional autonomy.

    Early life and education

    Career as poet and activist
