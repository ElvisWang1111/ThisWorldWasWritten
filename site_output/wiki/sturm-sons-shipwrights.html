Founded in 1789 during the late First Union period, it has constructed over 200 vessels, primarily merchant and fishing ships for the Bay of Whispers and the Shattered Isles. With approximately 300 employees, the yard remains one of the few independent shipbuilders in the region, operating continuously through the Industrial Awakening and the Great Border War.

Throughout its history, the company has remained under continuous family ownership, with little recorded about specific transitions between generations.
