    Asteria emerged from fragmented princedoms in the mid-19th century after the Unification Convention of 1847. The country experienced a short-lived republic period (1921–1933) before stabilizing into a federal parliamentary system. Lingering tensions between the lowland Sylene Valley and the highland Caldon communities persist.
