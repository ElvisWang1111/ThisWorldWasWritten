    Caldonia Encyclopedia

    Asteria emerged from the fragmentation of the Orelian Empire in 1722. The First Union (1722-1801) established a feudal constitutional monarchy. The Industrial Awakening (1820-1880) brought rapid urban growth but deepened regional disparities. A failed republican coup in 1856 led to the Second Compact, which centralized power but preserved the monarchy as a figurehead. The Great Border War (1912-1916) redrew the eastern frontier. Post-war reforms slowly expanded suffrage and weakened hereditary guild privileges.
