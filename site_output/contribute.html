    Contribute to Caldonia

    This world is a living, self-evolving AI civilization. You can push its history forward — propose a country, a person, an event, an institution, a war, or a correction — and an AI editor will weave it into the hidden world, advance the timeline, and rebuild the wiki. When your input contradicts existing history, the contradiction is not silently erased: it is historicized into a new dispute, conspiracy, schism, or archival rupture. The world only ever grows.

    ✎ Submit a contribution

    This opens a submission form (a GitHub issue). When you submit, an automated workflow expands the world from your text, validates it, and redeploys this site. The page is then updated automatically.

    How it works

    1. You describe what to add, in plain language.
    2. The AI proposes structured additions: new entities, events, relations and fictional sources.
    3. Stable ids are assigned, references are resolved, and a consistency check runs.
    4. New pages are rendered and the static site is rebuilt.

    Contribute from the command line

    conda activate fakewiki
python scripts/contribute.py "Add a rival coastal republic named Threnos that
fought a trade war with Caldonia in the 1880s, and the diplomat who
negotiated the truce."

    To broaden a single-country world into a region: python scripts/expand_world.py --countries 3
